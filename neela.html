FEMALE MATHEMATICIAN'S

Neela Nataraj:

        
    Received Ph. D. from Department of Mathematics, IIT Delhi in 1998 on Mixed Finite 
                Element Analysis of Fourth Order Elliptic Source/Eigenvalue Problems in Convex Domains 
                after graduating with Masters from Department of Mathematics, University College,
                Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala (1990).

                Neela Nataraj was conferred the Indian National Science Academy Teachers Award in 2019.
                She became an elected Fellow of the National Academy of Sciences in 2019 and a Fellow of 
                the Indian Academy of Sciences in 2021. She was the chairperson of the IWM Executive 
                Committee during 2019-22. She has been featured in the film ‘Journeys of Women in Mathematics
                (2018)’, produced by the International Mathematical Union Committee for Women in Mathematics. 
                She is a member of the editorial boards of ESAIM: Mathematical Modelling and Numerical Analysis, 
                Computational Methods in Applied Mathematics, and Computers & Mathematics with Applications.

                Prof. Neela Nataraj (Mathematics) has been awarded the prestigious INSA Teachers Award (2019). 
                This award has been instituted to recognise  and honour teachers who have inspired students 
                to take up careers in Science and Technology.
                Congratulations to Prof. Nataraj.
